Got questions?

Common questions

The things people usually ask.

How do commitment modes work?
+
Each mode adds a different layer of accountability. Chill is pure guidance — no pressure at all. Focus uses streaks to build habit. Serious and Hardcore are coming soon and will add financial or social stakes for people who want a real push.
What happens if I miss a task?
+
In Chill mode, nothing. The task waits for you. In Focus mode, your streak resets — but your saved work stays intact. You never lose your progress, only the streak count. Just pick up from where you left off.
